Who We Are

Enactus JOOUST is a student-led entrepreneurship team at Jaramogi Oginga Odinga University of Science and Technology. We design ventures that improve livelihoods, generate income, and strengthen local communities.

Our Mission

To cultivate entrepreneurial leaders who deliver sustainable impact through innovative community ventures.

Our Vision

A world where young innovators create inclusive prosperity and resilient, self-reliant communities.

Core Values

Leadership

We lead with integrity, accountability, and a focus on results.

Sustainability

We build ventures that last, creating long-term value for communities.

Innovation

We apply creativity and evidence to solve real-world challenges.

Enactus Global Affiliation

Enactus is a global network of university students, academics, and business leaders committed to using entrepreneurial action to improve lives. As Enactus JOOUST, we compete, collaborate, and learn with teams worldwide.
